# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This course bridges the gap between digital AI (the brain) and physical robotics (the body), enabling you to apply AI knowledge to control humanoid robots in both simulated and real-world environments.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to AI systems that operate in and interact with the physical world. Unlike traditional AI that exists purely in the digital realm, Physical AI must understand and navigate physical constraints, sensor data, and real-world uncertainties.

## Course Structure

This textbook is organized into 7 major chapters covering 12 weeks of instruction:

1. **Course Overview**: Learning outcomes, weekly breakdown, prerequisites
2. **Hardware & Infrastructure**: Workstation specs, edge kits, lab options, cloud alternatives
3. **ROS 2 Fundamentals**: Installation, nodes/topics, services, navigation, debugging
4. **Digital Twin Simulation**: Gazebo, Unity, URDF modeling, sensor integration, physics
5. **NVIDIA Isaac Platform**: Isaac Sim, Omniverse, robot brain AI, perception, SLAM
6. **Vision-Language-Action**: VLA models, multimodal learning, action primitives
7. **Humanoid Robotics**: Locomotion, manipulation, balance, conversational AI, HRI design

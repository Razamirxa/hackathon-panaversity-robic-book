# Human-Robot Interaction Design

Design principles for effective human-robot interaction.

## Design Principles

- Predictability
- Transparency
- Social cues
- Safety

## Interaction Modalities

- Speech
- Gestures
- Gaze
- Touch

## Summary

Good HRI design ensures safe and effective robot interactions.
